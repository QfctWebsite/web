The golf cart is the team’s oldest project. The vehicle is a 1980 Club Car DS Electric, and has been with QFCT since 2010, when it was retrofitted to run on alkaline fuel cells. In 2014, with sponsorship from Horizon Fuel Cell Technologies, we obtained a Polymer Electrolyte Membrane (PEM) fuel cell and have since converted the golf cart to run with a new hydrogen system, replacing the alkaline fuel cell system. This golf cart has been our greatest promotional vehicle that we take to all our events, promotional activities, and demonstrations.
